About Me

Designer turned coder

Introduction

I started designing for the web in the late 90s, while I was residing in Cyprus, pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Business Administration. Then, I created a first-ever website dedicated to my hometown of Bila Tserkva (Ukraine). Some years later, my project won a web design competition award.

Upon graduation and eventual return to Ukraine, I had a decision to make as to which career to pursue. My first ever professional job interview landed me a position of Web Developer for a b2b agency pulse.ua.

The rest, as they say, is history.
