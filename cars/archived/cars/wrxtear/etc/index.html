FAQ's & Tips

Problem: Enginuity ECU Logger; logs are outputting with all columns messed up, for instance RPM appears in a wrong column, etc. What to do?

Solution: Make sure your regional settings are set to United States. I had this problem when my computer's regional settings were set to Russia and logs were corrupt. As soon as I reset regional settings back to United States, logs appears in a normal form. Click here for the blog entry for this solution.

 

Problem: I can't get my computer to read ECU, computer says 'openport 1.3 subaru' driver could not be installed/found. Often times, if your computer is connected to internet it will be installed automatically. In my case, it did so on laptop, but carputer.

Solution: If this driver doesn't get installed automatically, you will need to download ECUExplorer, and that will solve the problem. openport 1.3 subaru is embedded in ECUExplorer. Sometimes, you will have to uninstall Enginuity and then install ECUExplorer. Then re-install Enginuity. Click here for more on this issue.
