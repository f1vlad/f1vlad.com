For archive/record reasons, some notes from original owner of the bigsparow.

Because of a careless driver with a small trailer not paying attention
on an off-ramp I wound up with a dented drivers fender (quite minor).  The upside of the repair was that it allowed me to get rid of some stone chips on the front bumper and leading edge of the hood - the paint on the nose of these cars chips very easily which you'll know if you've looked at a few.  This car now has a spottless nose (perfect paint match) making it one of the cleaner cars you'll find.  If you get to see the car I believe you'll agree.

Motor has always been stock including exhaust. The bezel was prepped and sprayed with Krylon to get rid of some of the bright silver - looks a little more low key and the Krylon appears to be as durable as the factory silver coating. No rust on the trunk lid or rest of the car.
I'm selling because I have too many cars at this point.
